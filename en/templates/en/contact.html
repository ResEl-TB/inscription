Contact a ResEl administrator

By using an IRC client

IRC is an instant chat protocol used by the ResEl administrators. This is the most effective way to contact an administrator.


By sending an email

If you don't want to use IRC, you can send us an email by using this form. You will get an answer as soon as an administrator see your email. Please complete all the fields.

This mail will be sent to inscription@resel.fr.

If you live in the MaisEl and you don't have an account on Télécom Bretagne, you can ask us to create your account on the ResEl database. You will need one in order to register your device on our inscription website and have an Internet access.
