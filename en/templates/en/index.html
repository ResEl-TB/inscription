Preliminaries

You just come to the ResEl and your device is not yet registered. Therefore you can not access to the Internet.

If you want to register, you have to pay a 25 € fee (settled in the ResEl policy and the status). Usually this fee is paid in October. You will receive further instructions in order to do that.

The only way to register your device is to be a ResEl member, as dexcribed in the ResEl policy.


How do I get my Internet access ?

Just go here. You will be asked to log in. Two options :

  • If you are a student or if you have a Télécom Bretagne account, you can log in with your school account, and your ResEl password. Then just follow the instructions.
  • If you are a trainee living in the MaisEl and you do not have a Télécom Bretagne account, please contact us.

Once your device is registered...

The above links will work only if you are registered.

Once registered, you will have your Internet access, but also some services offered by the ResEl. Many tutorials exist there, check them if you want.

If you have any questions, take a look at our ResEl FAQ. If you have any problem, please contact us.

Warning : once registered, please visit the post-inscription configuration page.
